    Devanna Rajeswari

    MBBS MRCPI FRCOG

    Consultant Gynaecologist & Obstetrician

    Miss Rajeswari has been a Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist since 2008. Her NHS practice is based at Ashford and St.Peter's Foundation Trust. She offers private consultations at:

    • The Runnymede Hospital, Chertsey

    • Princess Margaret Hospital, Windsor

    • Nuffield Hospital Woking

    • Prime Health Weybridge

    Miss Rajeswari trained in India and completed further training in Kenya and the Reupblic of Ireland. She continued her higher training in North West London and was appointed as a Consultant at St. Peter's Hospital in 2008.
